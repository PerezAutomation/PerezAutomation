About

Established in early 2026 in Melbourne, Australia, Perez Automation began as a personal engineering journal alongside the journey through a Bachelor of Mechatronics Engineering.

Founded by Valentino Perez Elizalde — born in Argentina and raised in Australia — this platform documents the path of a final-year engineering student specialising in Advanced Automation and Manufacturing.

Inspired by the world’s leading industrial designers, engineers, and innovators, this space explores ideas across automotive systems, robotics, production technologies, and emerging automation. Nothing is off the table.

Music from Muse, Radiohead, The Strokes, and Tool constantly fuels the background, while skateboarding, health, and discipline keep the mind and body aligned.

Purpose

Perez Automation exists to document ideas, projects, and learning experiences across automation, mechatronics, and modern engineering systems.
